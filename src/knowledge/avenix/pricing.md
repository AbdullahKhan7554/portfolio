# Pricing

## How Pricing Works

Every project gets a fixed quote before work begins. After a short scoping
conversation about your goals and scope, you receive a clear, all-in price — no
hourly surprises.

## Indicative Starting Points

- Launch (single page): from $300
- Business Website (5–7 pages): from $700
- Web Application: custom, quoted after scoping

## What Affects Price

- Number of pages and unique templates
- Integrations (bookings, payments, CRMs, maps)
- Custom functionality and content readiness
- Timeline and level of ongoing support

## Payment Terms

A deposit secures your start date; the balance is due on delivery. Terms are
confirmed in writing with the fixed quote.
