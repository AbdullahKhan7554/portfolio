# Process

## 1. Discover

We clarify your goals, audience, and scope, then agree a fixed quote and
timeline before any work starts.

## 2. Design

We shape the structure and visual direction — clean, premium, and built to
convert, not just to look good.

## 3. Build

Development in Next.js / MERN with performance, accessibility, and SEO baked in
from the first commit.

## 4. Harden

Quality pass: Core Web Vitals, accessibility (WCAG), technical SEO, and
cross-device testing.

## 5. Launch

We deploy, connect analytics and tracking, and hand over — with support options
if you want us to keep improving things.
