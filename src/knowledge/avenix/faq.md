# FAQ

## How long does a project take?

A Launch page is typically a few days to a week; a Business Website usually
takes 2–4 weeks depending on content readiness and scope.

## How much does a website cost?

You get a fixed quote before work begins. As a guide, Launch starts from $300
and a Business Website from $700; web apps are quoted after a short scoping call.

## Do you offer revisions?

Yes. Each project includes review rounds so we refine the details together
before launch.

## What do you need from us?

Your goals, brand assets (logo, images), and any copy you already have. If
content isn't ready, we provide structure and guidance.

## Do you work with international clients?

Yes — Avenix Studio works with clients worldwide and communicates in your
timezone-friendly windows.

## How do we get started?

Message on WhatsApp or use the contact form. We'll scope the work and send a
fixed quote.
